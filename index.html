The website will guide you through general information about the city, numerous activities you can check out as well as top restaurants to visit. Waterville Valley is a city in the White Mountains region of New Hampshire. The city was incorporated in 1829, and as of the 2020 census, the population was 508 (49% female and 51% male). The median age was 45 years old.
There were approximately 121 households, of which 27% had children under the age of 18 living with them, 46% were married couples living together and 35% were made up of individuals
The medium houshold income was $89,276 and the per capita income was $73,020.
